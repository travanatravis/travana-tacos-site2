What is the main order?

Travana is centered on one signature order: quesabirria tacos served as a box of three with consomé for dipping.

Do you have a large menu?

No. The menu stays intentionally tight so the line moves fast and the hero item stays consistent.

No. The current plan is card only for faster checkout and cleaner service flow.
