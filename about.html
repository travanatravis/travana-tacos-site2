About Travana

A love story, three boys, and one taco worth building a brand around.

Travana Tacos started with Travis and Ana Woods, a house full of boys who always say yes to tacos, and a refusal to keep settling for flavor that felt watered down or just plain wrong.

Our story

How Travana got here

2018
Travis and Ana meet in Northern Virginia — Travis in Ashburn, Ana in Manassas with her family, and tacos already very much part of the language.
Back in Rochester
Life fills up with marriage, three boys, and a lot of local meals that left Ana craving the real thing.
The running joke
Ana’s mom becomes the unofficial flavor judge with two legendary reviews: “¡Es diferente!” and “¡muchas azúcares!”
The turning point
After enough compromise, the answer becomes obvious: stop hunting, start cooking, and bring the flavor yourselves.
May 2026
Travana says hello at the Rochester Lilac Festival with one hero item and a very clear point of view.
Why only one taco?

Because the whole idea is to master the one thing that best represents the brand instead of diluting it with extras.

What should the first bite feel like?

Crispy outside, juicy inside, deep savory dip, and that instant “ohhh yea baby” moment.

Family flavor board

Travana is a love letter to the taco that kept missing from the local scene.

Everything about the brand is built to feel warm, bold, a little rebellious, and rooted in real family appetite — not generic “food truck” energy.

The whole point is for one taco to carry a whole story.

That story is Northern Virginia sparks, Mexican flavor roots, Rochester family life, and zero interest in watered-down shortcuts.

Welcome to the family

The brand is simple because the mission is simple.

Serve one unforgettable taco, keep it fast, keep it fair, and make Rochester feel the difference immediately.
