Our story

The Travana Origin

One taco. Done right. Built for speed. Priced for everyone.

Mastery is knowing what to leave out.

It all started in Northern Virginia back in 2018. Travis, a Rochester native working in IT for a military contract company, met Ana, who was visiting Manassas on a family vacation from Mexico. Fast forward a few years, and we’re now married, living in Rochester, and raising three boys. In our house, when the question of what to make for dinner comes up, tacos are basically always a YES.

But as much as we love our Rochester home, there was one glaring problem: the local taco scene wasn’t consistently hitting the mark. Ana was missing the authentic Mexican flavors she grew up with.

So we stopped searching for the perfect taco and decided to make it ourselves. We brought authentic flavor in full force, and Travana Tacos was born.
